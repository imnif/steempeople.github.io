full house with ginkgo trees

by @raah

가로 100cm 정도watercolor on paper

There are many ginkgo trees on every street in Korea.
Today’s watercolor painting is a full house with ginkgo trees this fall.
This house is made of mud. I built it with my family. There’s a grid of trees on top of the white plaster.
