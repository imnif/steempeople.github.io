[Drug wars] What you need to do now in Drug Wars.

That’s war!

Drug War is a War game

What you need to do to make the war vigorous

1

Make war points or war coins.
The war serves as a kind of (steem) miner.
it allows wars to happen more for other purposes. (incentives!)
A war coin is awarded to the warlord top10 every day(or every week)

2

Must make an item market
It is linked with No. 1.
It can only be purchased as a war point.

3

Safe amount should be reduced.
Otherwise, all will only invest in warehouse upgrades.
Limit the number of times you can heist.
If you purchase a specific item, you must make additional heist possible.

4

Need to dramatically improve find’s search system
(Current resource situation or military situation should also be indicated in search condition.)

5

It must have a guild system or declaration war function.
Group system(gangs ) should be created.
Group vs group war should be possible.

At the World War level, war outbreaks should occur several times a month.

6

It should be able to damage buildings as well as damage the troops during the war
