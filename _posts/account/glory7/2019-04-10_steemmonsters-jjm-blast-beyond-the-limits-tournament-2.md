**STEEMMONSTERS JJM BLAST: Beyond the Limits!**

JJM BLAST tournament #1 was successfully done last Sunday, with 110 players entered. We are announcing Tournament #2, which is this Sunday! (8am EST, 9pm KST).

* [Tournament Link](https://steemmonsters.com/?p=tournament&id=3f0e18b355c72ac3ed2ff8bbb476b0ae56c89c07)
