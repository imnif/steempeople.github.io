![IMG_3389.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmVRBXS8FiqBajqKfwaau5uEQ21gy55eQSbN3e7raBQf3Y/IMG_3389.JPG)

Last week, just before fine dust hit the Korean Peninsula, I went to Hansalim(kind of like Whole Foods Market). I walked to the place with my baby boy in his stroller which I use also as a shopping cart. It took me about an hour roundtrip.
>지난주 미세먼지가 기승을 부리기 전에 한살림까지 전용 카트를 끌고 걸어서 장보러 다녀왔다. 한살림이 집에서 조금 거리가 있어서 가는데 30분, 오는데 30분 걸리지만, 그래도 어김없이 전용 카트와 함께 힘차게!

![IMG_3392.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmaaQczDEjPZVkw1TQhvFzkHsbYAZMoXVpeEn7YHSScTBq/IMG_3392.JPG)

The stroller is always overloaded that you can’t really tell if it’s a stroller or a shopping cart.
>늘 유모차인지 짐차인지 알 수 없을 만큼 과적해서 돌아오는 길.

![IMG_3393.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmUCawAgfZoy2MhJS834X4VtrcXu7A7imT5aWGHusSgKKd/IMG_3393.JPG)

My hobby has been walking for such a long time since I was young and none of my girl friends seemed to like walking. So most of the times I had to walk by myself.
>예전부터 내 취미이자 유일한 스트레스 해소법은 걷기 였는데, 여자친구들은 이 취미를 함께해주지 않았기에 무척이나 외로웠던 취미였다. 보통 산책하러 나가면 나보다는 훨씬 더 연령대가 높으신 분들이 대부분이니 ^^;;

Fortunately, I now have a companion and walk so much with my baby in a stroller. I walk with the stroller so much that now I would even feel awkward to walk by myself without the stroller.
>다행히 아이들을 낳고 키우며 유모차를 끌고 이 취미를 원없이 누리고 있다. 이제는 유모차를 끌고 혼자 걷기가 어색할 정도로...😅

![IMG_3416.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmWdxwXSJ58fxRQqBPBJxV9FuUo4vKXdU5X3LsbgFyVn8B/IMG_3416.JPG)

These are the things I bought at Hansalim! Organic food! Yum :)
>그렇게 걸어서 직접 장봐온 물품들이 이렇게나 많다니!
